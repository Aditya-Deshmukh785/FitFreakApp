About FitFreak

FitFreak was born out of passion and purpose. It all began during the 2020 lockdown, when gyms were closed, and staying fit became a real challenge. Our founder, a certified gym trainer from Yavatmal, Maharashtra, realized how many people were struggling to stay consistent and motivated from home.

What started as a few virtual fitness sessions quickly grew into a full-blown online platform. With over 5 years of hands-on training experience, the vision was simple — to bring expert-guided fitness plans to everyone, no matter where they are.

From personalized diet charts to smart workout splits, FitFreak has grown into a trusted name in the fitness community. We believe that fitness isn't just a goal — it's a lifestyle. Today, along with our online platform, we proudly run an offline gym in Yavatmal, Maharashtra, where the FitFreak journey first began.

Whether you're starting out or pushing your limits — FitFreak is here to guide, transform, and elevate your fitness journey.
